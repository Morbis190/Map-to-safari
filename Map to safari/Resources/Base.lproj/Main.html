Google to Apple Maps Safari

This extension automatically converts Google Maps links to Apple Maps, making navigation seamless on your Apple devices.

Key Features:

  • One-click conversion from Google Maps to Apple Maps
  • Works automatically in the background
  • Preserves all location information
  • Native Apple Maps integration

How It Works

Once enabled, the extension will:

  1. Detect when you click a Google Maps link
  2. Convert the coordinates and location data
  3. Open the location in Apple Maps instead

Privacy First

This extension respects your privacy:

  • No data collection or tracking
  • Works completely offline
  • No personal information stored

You can turn on Google to Apple Maps Safari's Safari extension in Settings.
